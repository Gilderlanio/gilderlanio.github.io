Softwares and Biological Databases

mtDNA variant calling pipeline

An mtDNA variant calling pipeline is a bioinformatics workflow used to detect mutations in mitochondrial DNA from sequencing data. It typically involves quality control, alignment to a mitochondrial reference genome, and variant detection to identify SNVs, indels, and heteroplasmy for research or clinical analysis.
