Software and databases

DANCE
Disease-Ancestry Networks (DANCE) comprise GWAS-hits, human complex phenotypes and population genetic variability in a Network-based approach.
AmazonForest
AmazonForest aims to improve user experience towards pathogenicity prediction. AmazonForest implements a Random forest model performs meta-prediction of pathogenicity of VUS, CI, and new genetic variants.
The mtDNA mutation-cancer network
The mtDNA mutation-cancer network is a bipartite network of mutations and cancer based on two distinct datasets (references below). The mutation-cancer network is a graph G(V, E), where V comprises two distinct sets of nodes: mitochondrial mutations (u) and cancers (v).
Datasets
The DeepHP database comprises 394,926 histopathological images, of which ~111K were labeled as Helicobacter pylori positive and ~283K remained as Helicobacter pylori-negative. Our dataset evidenced high potential and application in the computational pathology field and contributes to emerging deep learning-based applications in precision medicine filling a gap in obtaining well-annotated data for deep learning applications.
